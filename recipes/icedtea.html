Homemade Iced Tea

Description

This recipe provides a simple guide on how to make a fresh homemade iced tea

It's very easy, so I hope you can follow this steps well. Before you start, make sure that you have everything prepared.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 3 orange pack of tea bags
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large saucepan. Remove from heat and add tea bags. Cover and steep for 1 hour.
  2. Combine tea and sugar in a large pitcher. Stir until sugar dissolves; stir in lemon juice. Refrigerate until chilled.
  3. Enjoy your drink!
